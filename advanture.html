Advanture activity in Bihar

01. Kayaking in Ganga River

Paddle Along the smoothly gushing waves of Ganga in a comfy kayak. From a covered deck to a small place for the rafter to sit in, all the safety facilities are offered by the kayak schools around here. Before the start of this activity, proper basic insights as well as techniques will be provided to you by the instructor, after which you are free to take your kayak along the Gandhi Setu and traverse through the sparkling river Ganga. The serenity of the ghats all around and the unpredictability of the flow of Ganges makes it one of the best adventure things to do in Bihar.

"Location: Gandhi Setu, Patna, Bihar"

02. Paramotoring in Patna

One of the most famous adventure things to do in Bihar is enjoying a session of paramotoring. If you’re looking for a day filled with thrill then you can take up this activity and fly 500 m above in the sky. Just like paragliding, paramotoring also lets you take a flight in the sky in which you float along various landmarks while being settled in a motor like a vehicle. The back of this vehicle will have a thrust motor connected to it and the running motor will take this parachute high in the sky. The activity is safe and you will be guided by professionals before starting it as well as proper gears will be provided to ensure your safety.

Location: Gangajal, Patna

03. Rafting in Valmiki Nagar

Famed for its tiger reserve, Valmiki Nagar has been a host to a variety of wildlife as well as different species of flora. Having river Gandhak flowing through it, this place has also made rafting quite a go to activity. Experience the sudden rush of adrenaline as you take your raft towards the thrilling streams of Gandhak River and traverse through the topsy turvy passages. You can take your raft along the borders of Triveni to enjoy the panoramic views of the lush green nature. All these factors when combined make rafting in Valmiki Nagar one of the most preferred adventure things to do in Bihar.

Location: Valmiki Nagar, Bettiah

04. Hot Air Balloon in Patna

Soar high in the sky by availing a session of hot air ballooning along the skies of Parna. Get a chance to enjoy a birds’ eye view of the whole town for more than ten minutes. You will be floating across many famous tourist attractions of Patna and will get a chance to get your pictures clicked as well. The height upto which you will be floating will remain around 100 metres from the ground, thus panoramic views of the surroundings are guaranteed. Among all the exhilarating adventure things to do in Bihar, this activity has attracted most of the tourists.

Location: Gangajal, Patna

05. Trek to Rajgir

Preached at a height of 73 m above the ground, the fort of Rajgir is known for being home to the world‘s first university. This trekking adventure will not only take you to the most beautiful location of Bihar but will also take you back to the past through its various ruins and cyclopean walls. Often considered to be an offbeat trek, the path to Rajgir is loaded with hilly slopes, boulder laden ridges and small sprouting of water. The area where you have to trek along the cyclopean wall has made it one of the most enjoyable adventure things to do in Bihar due to the narrow pathways and broken ladders.

Location: Patna, Bihar

06. Go Karting in Patna

Go-karting in Patna is one of those activities which can be done by almost everyone. The activity lets you take around six rounds of 1 km each around the racing track in which the speed needs to be limited. Not only do you experience the thrill of gushing wind along your ears but you also experience the once-in-a-lifetime experience of racing with your fellows without any issue. Other than all this, many basic safety features are followed here and you are instructed by professionals before starting the race.

Location: Gangajal, Patna

07. River Ganga Cruise

Providing you with a chance to experience the serenity of the river Ganga from a different perspective, a session of cuisine along the river is a must-try activity here. Considered to be one of the best fun and adventure things to do in Bihar, this activity not only lets you enjoy the panoramic views of surrounding tourist landmarks including Darbhanga House, Gandhi Setu, Gaighat and others, but also lets you take part in rituals and prayers.

Location: Gandhi Ghat, Patna

08. Visit Kakolat Waterfall

Gushing from a height of 160 feet from the ground level, the Kakolat waterfall is known for the Hindu mythology related to it. The water here remains cold throughout the year and the stream below creates a big pool which is open for visitors to enjoy a dip. To enjoy the best of it, make sure that you visit here around monsoon as this is the time when it is at its maximum thrust. Since the waterfall lies in the middle of a lush green forest, the overall beauty of this waterfall and the surrounding medicinal plants make visiting here a must to activity.

Location: Rajgir City, Bihar

09. Jeep Safari in Valmiki Tiger Reserve

Head out on a four cross four army jeep safari and traverse through the most dangerous parts in the forest. While Valmiki Tiger reserve is famous for being a home to more than 26 tigers, spotting them is never a simple task. Other than tigers, visitors might also spot various species of animals like elephants, deers and leopards while enjoying a safari. The whole forest is covered with Champa trees , therefore making it difficult to spot animals. However, if you are guided by a local, you will be driven towards the most famous sports known for animal sightings.

Location: Valmiki Nagar, Bettiah

12. Experience Jungle Safari at Udaypur Wildlife Sanctuary

Covering up an area of 8.74 km Square, the Udaypur wildlife sanctuary lies along the foothills of Gandak river. Go to spot for the migratory birds as well as animals this wildlife sanctuary lets people enjoy the sights of various different species resting in their natural habitat. While opting for a jungle safari you will be taken through various animal spotting locations and if lucky enough you might get a chance to spot barking deer, Paul, nilgai, wild boar as well as spotted deer. 13. Rajgir Day Hiking from Patna, Bihar

Gridhakuta is a hill located at Rajgir, Bihar where Lord Buddha carried out many dialogues with his disciples after he achieved enlightenment. It is the place where Lord Buddha started his second helm of commandment and preached many inspiring teachings and sermons to his disciples. Gridhakuta hill is also known as Vulture’s Peak. It was at Gridhakuta hill that Lord Buddha gave his two significant sutras the Lotus Sutra and the Prajnaparamita.

Starting point/Endpoint: Your hotel or residence in Patna City

14. Scuba diving in Patna

Get ready to explore the deep waters of the Ganges while enjoying the session of scuba-diving. You will be guided by the professionals who will offer you all safety equipment and will take you to the most beautiful places within the water. This activity will not only let you indulge in an adventure of a lifetime but will also let you explore Ganga from a whole different perspective. You will be surrounded by colourful fishes as well as various species of turtles. No matter how rare this activity may seem considering Bihar’s water streams, Scuba is still one of the most adventurous things to do in Bihar.

The stream of water creates a pool below it around which one can spot various medicinal plants. From taking a dip into the water to enjoying a session of hiking around it, one can indulge in various activities here.
